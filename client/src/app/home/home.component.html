Slot Booking Assistant

Welcome,

 Slot Booking Assistant is a full-fledged online event booking system that will assist you to book Classrooms, Auditoriums and Conference Rooms at particular timeslots for your events or lectures. It has simple user interface and administration page that gives you the ability to send a request containing details of your event to the Admin. Admin has the authority to accept/deny the events according to the preference. All the wonderful events happening at what place and at what time, is displayed in your calender. All the history of your past events is maintaind in this. Let us assist you with your event bookings.
